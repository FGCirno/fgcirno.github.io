What I've Been Cooking

I finally pushed out a pretty significant visual overhaul to this place. I'm pretty pleased with the way it looks and have to thank Bell a bunch for helping significantly with his decades of coding experience he has above me.

I've also been working on a bit of a secondary project of mine. I mentioned before that I'm an aspiring YouTuber. Well, I've finished recording for my first video I'm planning on making. It's going to be on the 2004 Nippon Ichi Software game Phantom Brave (specifically using footage from the Steam rerelease by the lovely people behind DSFix). I have a lot to say about it and the script for that video is presently 8,991 words long and counting. I'm about half way done with it. I'm hoping it's not too big of an undertaking, but it's a bit of a passion project of mine and I hope it gets more eyes on Phantom Brave.

Other videos I have planned are an essay about how interesting debut games are compared to series that go on for a long time (see: Sonic, Kingdom Hearts, Devil May Cry), a video comparing in depth Omori and Deltarune (not original but a video like that hasn't been made since Chapter 2 was the newest part of Deltarune), and a video on Yoshiro Kimura's many video games.
